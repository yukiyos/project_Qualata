How it works
Three steps from raw data to actionable insight.
01

Upload your CSV

Drag in any CSV file or load the built-in mock dataset to get started immediately. No schema definition needed.

02

Run the analysis

Qualata calculates completeness, uniqueness, and validity scores, then optionally asks your local Ollama model for a narrative explanation.

03

Explore & visualise

Switch to the Visualise tab — or click "Send to Visualise" after analysis — to generate charts from your columns instantly.
